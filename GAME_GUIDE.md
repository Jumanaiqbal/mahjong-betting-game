# 🀄 MAHJONG BETTING GAME - Complete Guide

## Game Overview
The Mahjong Betting Game is a fast-paced card prediction game where you bet whether the next hand's total value will be **HIGHER** or **LOWER** than your current hand.

---

## 📊 The Cards (Tiles)

### Total: **34 Mahjong Tiles**

#### 1️⃣ **NUMBER TILES** (27 tiles) - Fixed Values
These tiles have their face value and **NEVER change**:

- **🀐-🀘 BAMBOO SUIT** (1-9 Bamboo)
  - 1 Bamboo = value 1
  - 2 Bamboo = value 2
  - ...up to...
  - 9 Bamboo = value 9

- **🀇-🀏 CHARACTERS SUIT** (1-9 Characters)  
  - 1 Characters = value 1
  - 2 Characters = value 2
  - ...up to...
  - 9 Characters = value 9

- **🀙-🀡 DOTS SUIT** (1-9 Dots)
  - 1 Dots = value 1
  - 2 Dots = value 2
  - ...up to...
  - 9 Dots = value 9

#### 2️⃣ **DRAGON TILES** (3 tiles) - Dynamic Values (Start: 5)
These tiles change value based on wins/losses:

- 🀄 **Red Dragon** (starts at value 5)
- 🀅 **Green Dragon** (starts at value 5)
- 🀆 **White Dragon** (starts at value 5)

#### 3️⃣ **WIND TILES** (4 tiles) - Dynamic Values (Start: 5)
These tiles change value based on wins/losses:

- 🀀 **East Wind** (starts at value 5)
- 🀁 **West Wind** (starts at value 5)
- 🀂 **North Wind** (starts at value 5)
- 🀃 **South Wind** (starts at value 5)

## 🎮 How to Play

### Step 1: **See Your Hand**
- You draw **3 tiles** at the start of each round
- The center panel shows:
  - 🎴 The tiles with their emojis
  - 📝 Their names
  - #️⃣ Their current values
  - **TOTAL:** The sum of all 3 tile values

### Step 2: **Make Your Bet**
Click one of two buttons:
- **⬆️ BET HIGHER** (Green) - Next hand's total will be HIGHER
- **⬇️ BET LOWER** (Red) - Next hand's total will be LOWER

### Step 3: **See the Result**
- The game draws a new 3-tile hand
- Compares the totals
- Shows result: **✓ Win!** (green), **✗ Lose!** (red), or **○ Tie!** (yellow)

### Step 4: **Repeat**
- Keep betting and trying to get a high score!
- Game ends when:
  - Any Dragon or Wind tile reaches value 0 or 10
  - Draw pile has been reshuffled 3 times

---

## 📈 Scoring System

### Points Per Round
- **WIN**: You earn points equal to your NEW hand's total value
  - Example: You win with new hand total 24 → +24 points
- **LOSE or TIE**: You earn 0 points

### Final Score Bonuses
When the game ends:
1. **Base Score**: All points earned from wins
2. **+Streak Bonus**: (Consecutive Wins × 10)
   - Example: 3 streak → +30 bonus
3. **+Round Bonus**: (Rounds Played × 2)
   - Example: 15 rounds → +30 bonus

**Final Score = Base + Streak Bonus + Round Bonus**

---

## 🃏 Dynamic Tile Values

### How Dragon/Wind Tiles Change

**When You WIN:**
- Dragon/Wind tiles in your NEW hand → **+1 value**
- They become stronger for next round!

**When You LOSE:**
- Dragon/Wind tiles in your NEW hand → **-1 value**  
- They become weaker for next round!

**When You TIE:**
- No change to any tile values

### Value Boundaries
- **Minimum**: 0 (Game Over!)
- **Maximum**: 10 (Game Over!)
- Number tiles stay 1-9 forever

## 🎯 Game Interface

### Top Bar (Game Stats)
- **Score**: Your current total points
- **Streak 🔥**: Consecutive wins (resets to 0 on loss/tie)
- **Draw**: Remaining cards in draw pile (starts: 31)
- **Discard**: Cards you've already played (starts: 0)
- **Reshuffles**: How many times draw pile was reshuffled (Max: 3)

### Middle Section (3 Columns)

**LEFT: Draw Pile 🔷**
- Shows stacked face-down cards
- Displays remaining card count
- These cards will be drawn for next round

**CENTER: Your Current Hand** ⭐ (MOST IMPORTANT)
- Shows 3 tiles with emojis
- Shows each tile's name and value
- Shows TOTAL value (sum of 3 tiles)
- Used to decide your bet!

**RIGHT: Discard Pile 🔶**
- Top card shown (if any)
- Card count below
- These are cards you already played

### Bottom Section

**Bet Buttons** 
- ⬆️ GREEN button = "Next hand will be HIGHER"
- ⬇️ RED button = "Next hand will be LOWER"

**Recent Hands Strip**
- Shows last 5 rounds
- Green border = Won
- Red border = Lost
- Yellow border = Tie
- Small tile emojis and values

---

## 📋 Game Over Conditions

Game ends when ANY of these happen:

1. **Tile Value Boundary Hit**
   - Dragon or Wind tile reaches value 10 → TOO HIGH
   - Dragon or Wind tile reaches value 0 → TOO LOW

2. **Reshuffled 3 Times**
   - When draw pile empty → reshuffle the discard pile
   - Max 3 reshuffles allowed
   - On 3rd reshuffle completion → Game Over

---

## 💡 Strategy Tips

1. **Watch Dragon/Wind tiles** - They're the game changers! Track their values.
2. **High Score Hands** - Bet higher after you win to keep the streak!
3. **Tile Patterns** - Remember: Number tiles are stable (1-9), only Dragons/Winds change
4. **Streak Building** - Consecutive wins = Major bonus! Risk it!
5. **Safe Plays** - All-number-tile hands = Predictable values

## 🎪 Example Game Flow

```
ROUND 1:

Status:      White Dragon stays at 5

Your hand:   🀘(9) + 🀏(9) + 🀞(6) = 24
You bet:     ⬇️ LOWER
Next hand:   🀇(1) + 🀅(5) + 🀂(5) = 11
Result:      ✗ LOSE! (11 < 24)
Points:      +0
Streak:      💔 RESET to 0
Green Dragon: 5 - 1 = 4 (was in losing hand)
North Wind:  5 - 1 = 4 (was in losing hand)
```
